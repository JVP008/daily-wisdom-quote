# Privacy Policy for Daily Wisdom Quote

**Last updated:** December 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in **Daily Wisdom Quote** (the "Application").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to respect your privacy.
- **We do not collect any personal data.**
- The Application does not require you to create an account.
- Your preferences (such as liked quotes or theme settings) are stored locally on your device using `AsyncStorage`.

## 2. Local Storage

We use local storage on your device to:
- Save your "Liked" quotes.
- Persist your preferred application theme (Dark/Light mode).
- Save meditation timer preferences.

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers by us.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The Application may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
- **Expo:** As this app is built with Expo, it may collect some anonymous usage data to help improve the Expo platform.
- **Google Play Services:** Used for application distribution and monitoring.

Link to privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the app:
- [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Expo](https://expo.dev/privacy)

## 4. Log Data

In a case of an error in the app, we may collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

## 5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
